Enhancing Nexus Property Search

Project information

  • Category: Web design
  • Client: Valocity Global
Background

Nexus recently entered the real estate sector with a goal of offering innovative solutions to its clients. However, during a recent review of their property search, it became apparent that while it provided robust market research and property analysis features, it lacked integrated prospecting tools and defaulted filters, leading to a somewhat overwhelming user experience. This case study outlines the process of enhancing Nexus' property search to include prospecting tools alongside improved default filters, aiming to streamline the user experience and provide comprehensive support for real estate professionals.

Identifying Challenges

  1. Lack of Prospect Tools Nexus' property search primarily focuses on market research and property analysis, neglecting the crucial aspect of prospecting. Real estate professionals rely on prospecting to identify potential leads and convert them into clients.
  2. Overwhelming Interface The absence of default filters leads to an overwhelming user experience. Users are bombarded with vast amounts of data, making it challenging to narrow down their search parameters effectively.

Approach

  1. Research and Analysis Conduct in-depth market research and user interviews to understand the specific prospecting needs of real estate professionals. Identify common pain points and preferences regarding prospecting tools.
  2. Integration of Prospect Tools Collaborate with software developers to integrate prospecting tools seamlessly into Nexus' property search.
  3. Refinement of Default Filters Analyse user behaviour and preferences to determine the most commonly used search parameters. Implement default filters based on this analysis to simplify the search process and improve user experience.

Implementation

  1. Prospecting Tools Integration Extend the property search to allow lead generation. Enable users to identify potential leads based on specific criteria such as last sale and listing details, location, property type, and budget. Showcase result on the platform include summary statistics and data visualisation to aid in agents understanding of current market trends
  2. Default Filters Enhancement Implement default filters based on user feedback and data analysis. These filters should cover essential search parameters such as property type, location, price range, and amenities. Users can further customise their search criteria as needed.

Outcome

  1. Improved User Experience The integration of prospecting tools and default filters significantly enhances the user experience of Nexus' property search. Real estate professionals can now efficiently identify potential leads within a streamlined interface.
  2. Increased Efficiency With access to comprehensive prospecting tools, real estate professionals can save time and effort in the lead generation process.
  3. Competitive Advantage By offering a comprehensive property search platform that addresses the prospecting needs of real estate professionals, Nexus gains a competitive edge in the market. Clients are more likely to choose Nexus for their real estate needs due to the platform's effectiveness and user-friendly interface.

Conclusion

By recognizing the importance of prospecting alongside market research and property analysis, Nexus will successfully enhance its property search to meet the evolving needs of real estate professionals. The integration of prospecting tools and default filters will not only improve the user experience but also enhances efficiency and competitiveness in the market.

My Learnings

If I were to revisit this project, I would streamline the focus solely on enhancing prospecting tools within the property search functionality, without expanding the scope to include default filter improvements. The decision to narrow the scope arises from the recognition that expanding the project's scope too broadly can overwhelm the tech team and potentially compromise the quality and timeliness of deliverables. By concentrating efforts on improving prospecting tools, we can maintain a clear and manageable objective, allowing for more focused research, development, and implementation. This approach ensures that the project remains feasible within the constraints of available resources and expertise, ultimately maximising the likelihood of success while minimising undue strain on the tech team.
